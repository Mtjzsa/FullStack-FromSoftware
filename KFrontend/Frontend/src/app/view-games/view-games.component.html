About Elden Ring

Elden Ring is an upcoming action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It is being created by game director Hidetaka Miyazaki and author George R. R. Martin, known for his work on the A Song of Ice and Fire series.

Gameplay

Elden Ring features an open-world environment with a dynamic weather system and day-night cycle. Players can explore the world on horseback or on foot, encountering various enemies and non-playable characters along the way. The game retains the challenging combat and intricate level design that FromSoftware is known for.
